I N F I N I T Y

INFINITY

Our favorite store this month is the multibrand store INFINITY. You can find INFINITY at three different locations. In the center of Bergen you'll find the store at the shopping mall Galleriet. They are also represented at Lagunen and Horisont. Here you can find awesome Scandinavian brands such as Samsøe Samsøe, Arnie Says, Holzweiler, By Malene Birger, By Malina, Maud and many, many more. Scroll down to the map to see which INFINITY store is the closest to you, and happy shopping!
